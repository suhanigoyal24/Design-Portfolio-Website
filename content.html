How to Build Your Portfolio

1. Personal Information

Start with your basic details. This is the first thing visitors will see on your portfolio.

  • Use your full professional name
  • Choose a clear, professional title
  • Upload a high-quality headshot
  • Write a compelling bio (3-5 sentences)

2. Skills Section

Showcase your key skills that are relevant to your profession.

  • List technical skills first (e.g., HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Include soft skills (e.g., Communication, Teamwork)
  • Limit to 8-12 most relevant skills
  • Order by proficiency level

3. Work Experience

Detail your professional journey with relevant work experiences.

  • Start with your most recent position
  • Include company name and your job title
  • Add dates of employment
  • Describe responsibilities and achievements
  • Use bullet points for readability

4. Final Touches

Polish your portfolio before publishing.

  • Preview on different devices
  • Check for spelling/grammar errors
  • Ask for feedback from colleagues
  • Update your portfolio regularly
